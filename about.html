About

Pixel Puppets is a new collaboration between Ben Carlin and Joe Duggan. Ben is a creative director and technologist who co-founded Megaverse and led Game Changers, winner of the Broadcast Tech Innovation Award for Best Use of Virtual Production. Joe is a producer and head of digital at the National Youth Theatre, where he delivered the UK's first large-scale VR theatre show and is pioneering a partnership with Rineyard to explore the future of live storytelling and generative AI.

Think real-time engines, motion capture, live actors, and a dose of visual effects all tossed into one chaotic tech soup. It's puppetry for the 21st century—wild, unpredictable, and hijacked by the crowd in the room. Every theatre show, college gig, film bit, disco, or event brings the visuals, and twist the characters on stage in real time. If you're after polite puppet plays, you're in the wrong place. We prefer our theatre unfiltered, unhinged, and not even slightly sorry about it. Step right up—things are about to get weird.
